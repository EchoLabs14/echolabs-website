Creator FAQ

Good questions, straight answers.

Do I need a big following to join?
Nope. We cast for fit, not follower counts. Nano and UGC creators with zero audience get matched to briefs every week. What matters is trust, craft, and the right niche.
How does payment work?
Every campaign is paid. Rates and scope are agreed up front in writing before you create anything. No exposure deals, no surprises. You're paid on the terms of each brief.
What kind of content will I make?
It depends on the brief: short-form video, UGC for paid ads, founder collaborations, long-term ambassador work. We share the concept and creative direction; you bring your voice.
How often will I hear about campaigns?
Only when there's a real match. We'd rather send you three relevant briefs a quarter than spam you daily. Joining the network puts you in the pool; fit decides the rest.
Is there any cost to join?
None. Joining the EchoLabs Creator Network is free. We make money from brands, not creators.
